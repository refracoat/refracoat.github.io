# Thermal Insulation

Installation, maintenance, and repair of refractory linings

## Service Overview
- Definition and Purpose
- Benefits: heat loss reduction, energy efficiency, safety
- Type of work: new installation, maintenance and repair, emergency breakdown service, periodic inspections, refractory material supply

## Applications
- Kilns and furnaces
- Boilers and incinerators
- Reactors and vessels
- Ladles and tundishes
- Other high-temperature equipment

## Materials
- Castables
- Bricks
- Ceramics
- Fiber films

## Capabilities
- Engineering and design support
- Heat loss calculations
- Material selection consultation
- Installation methods and equipment
