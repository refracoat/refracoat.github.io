# Surface Protection

Protective rubber linings for equipment and material handling

## Service Overview
- Definition and Purpose
- Benefits: abrasion resistance, corrosion protection, impact absorption
- Type of work: new lining installation, re-lining and repairs, wear assessment and monitoring, emergency repair service

## Applications
- Chutes and hoppers
- Pipes and pipelines
- Tanks and vessels
- Screens and crushers
- Cyclones and separators
- Pump casings

## Materials
- Natural rubber compounds
- Synthetic rubber options
- Wear-resistant formulations
- Chemical-resistant formulations
- Temperature ranges

## Capabilities
- Cold bonding
- Hot vulcanization
- Spray-applied coatings
- Surface preparation requirements
