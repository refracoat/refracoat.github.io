# Special Conveyor Systems

Custom conveyor design and installation for harsh environments

## Service Overview
- Definition and Purpose
- Benefits: durability, reliability, and minimal maintenance
- Type of work: custom design and engineering, fabrication and installation, commissioning and training, maintenance and spare parts, upgrades and modifications

- High-temperature environments
- Abrasive material transport
- Chemical processing facilities
- Mining and mineral processing

- Screw conveyors
- Drag conveyors
- Custom engineered systems

- Heavy-duty drive systems
- Impact beds and load zones
- Tracking systems
- Dust suppression integration

- Wear component specification
